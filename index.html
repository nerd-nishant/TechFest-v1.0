TechXposium '24

    TechXposium is designed as flagship technical fest of SCE Supaul. The TechXposium is being organized by the Technical Club under the supervision of Faculty Advisors. This festival will become center of vibrant gatherings of innovation and brilliant minds composed of students,professionals and enthusiasts to engage in a variety of activities and showcase advancements in technology. Overall,the fest is planned as dynamic event that will celebrate technological innovation, encourages learning, collaboration, and provide a platform for showcasing talent and forging valuable connections within the tech community.
